COVID-19

As an educational website, why not learn a bit about the reason that inspired this website - COVID-19?

What is it?

  • COVID-19 is caused by a coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2
  • It's similar to SARs-CoV and other coronaviruses, but has many mutations
  • In humans, they can cause mild to severe repiratory infections

Symptoms

  • Incubation period is about 4-5 days (but could range from 2-14 days)
  • Symptoms may include fever, cough, breathing difficulties, fatigue, aches, headaches, diarrhea, nausea, or sore throat
  • Older people or those with underlying conditions like heart disease, lung disease, or diabetes, tend to be at higher risk to Covid-19

Spread

  • Virus spreads mainly from person to person (which is why 6 feet of social distancing is encouraged or mandated)
  • Can spread through respiratory droplets (talking, coughing, sneezing)
  • Virus could be on surfaces and can spread to a person upon touch
  • Some people may not show symptoms and still spread it

Science behind Covid-19 infection

  • Long, spiky proteins on virus' surface bind to our cells' ACE2 protein
  • The virus and the cell then crash, allowing the virus' genetic material to enter the cell
  • Then, it uses our cell like a factory and multiplies, eventually infecting many other cells, causing the infection

Precautions

  • Minimum of 6 feet social distancing between people
  • Wear masks when you are outside
  • Wash your hands often
  • Disinfect commonly touched surfaces
